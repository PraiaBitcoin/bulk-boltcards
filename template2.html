bitcoin recovery wallet

If you lose access to your wallet, you can request to restore the funds associated with your bolt card, paying a fee of 5000 SATs. Open a ticket at the paid support, explain why you need to restore and inform your card UID. The funds will be send only to the address associated with your mnemonic phrases listed bellow.

There is no way to recover the words, so, please take note and store in a safe place with your card UID. NEVER SHARE WITH ANYONE!
